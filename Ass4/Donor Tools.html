Tools for Giving

There are many questions to answer before you give, including where and how to donate. Here, you will find the tools you need to give with confidence.

Giving Basket

The Giving Basket empowers you to support multiple charities in one convenient checkout, while controlling how much of your personal information is shared with each organization.

Volunteer Opportunities

Charitable organizations don’t run on money alone. Find out where you can step in to support the important work of nonprofits by donating your time.

Giving Circle Finder

Giving Circles are a great way to connect with other donors to collectively support charities - creating an impact that is greater than the sum of its parts.

Estate Planner

Having a will or trust offers you peace of mind while protecting your loved ones, planning for your assets, and establishing a charitable legacy for your favorite causes.
