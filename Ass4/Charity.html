Our mission is to make impactful giving easier for all

Our mission at Charity NAV is to simplify impactful giving for all. We understand the challenges of choosing the right charitable organizations. That's why we evaluate and assess nonprofits to provide comprehensive ratings. With our transparent and reliable information, you can make informed decisions about where to contribute. Charity NAV goes beyond ratings – we are a community united by the goal of creating a better world. Join us in transforming the way we give and discover organizations aligned with your values. Together, we can make a significant difference. Explore Charity NAV today and unleash the power of your giving.
